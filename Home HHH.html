WHAT IS IT?

The 'Handy Hurley Hanger' started as an idea from the Ballynoe National School 5th and 6th class
as part of the Junior Entrepenuer Programme.





The Junior Entrepreneur Programme (JEP) Is a programme aimed at schools in Ireland
trying to promote entrepreneurship among children in Ireland. Two years ago, our school took
part in the Junior Entrepreneur Programme with 'Magic Mugs', a mug with the club/county crest on it.
We got to go to the JEP Exhibition in the RDS in Dublin. This year, we are taking part in the JEP again, this time
with the 'Handy Hurley Hangers', a locally sourced, durable and reasonably priced wooden stand to
hold your hurleys!
